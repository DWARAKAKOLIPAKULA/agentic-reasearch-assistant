Company Policy Handbook — Remote Work Guidelines (Internal Knowledge Base)

1. Remote Work Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period (90 days) are
eligible to apply for remote work arrangements. Approval is subject to manager
discretion and role requirements.

2. Working Hours
Remote employees are expected to be available during core collaboration hours,
10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time, regardless of their chosen working schedule.
Total working hours per week remain 40 hours.

3. Equipment and Reimbursement
The company provides a one-time equipment allowance of up to $500 for home office
setup, covering items such as monitors, chairs, and keyboards. Internet
reimbursement of $30/month is available upon submission of a utility bill.

4. Security Requirements
All remote employees must use company-issued VPN software when accessing internal
systems. Personal devices used for work must have endpoint protection software
installed and approved by the IT Security team.

5. Performance Reviews
Remote employees are evaluated using the same performance criteria as in-office
staff. Quarterly check-ins are mandatory and must be conducted via video call.

6. Travel Policy for Remote Employees
Remote employees required to travel to the head office for quarterly meetings
will have travel and accommodation expenses reimbursed, provided they are booked
through the approved company travel portal at least 14 days in advance.

7. Termination of Remote Arrangement
The company reserves the right to revoke remote work privileges with 30 days
written notice if performance metrics are not met for two consecutive quarters,
or if business needs require a return to office.
